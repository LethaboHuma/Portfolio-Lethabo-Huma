Visual Multimedia


I am a self-taught artist with five years of experience and currently in my final year of a multimedia degree. My work spans various creative disciplines, combining traditional art techniques with modern technology, including digital art, photography, and front-end development.

I specialize in semi-realistic portraiture and offer freelance services. I use generative AI to create unique references, enhancing the quality and individuality of each digital artwork.

Photography

My photography focuses on landscapes and scenes from my travels. I capture the natural beauty of the world, presenting it through a creative and thoughtful lens.

Traditional Art

In traditional art, I work with semi-realism, using mediums like pencil sketching, acrylic painting, and embellishment. My approach blends traditional and modern techniques to create textured, layered artworks.
