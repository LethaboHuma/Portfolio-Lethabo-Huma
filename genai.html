Generative AI Experimentation


For the past two years, experimenting with Generative AI models like Stable Diffusion, ComfyUI, Midjourney, and DALL-E has been a rewarding experience. Each model offers unique capabilities and challenges, and I've included some of my favorite outputs here.

Stable Diffusion & ComfyUI

Stable Diffusion and ComfyUI provide flexibility in prompt creation with a wide range of models to choose from. The challenge lies in the learning curve, from installation to troubleshooting bugs, but the ability to save results directly on the device is a significant advantage.



Midjourney

Midjourney offers a user-friendly interface but comes with higher costs and limited model variety. Achieving the desired results can be straightforward, but the lack of diversity in models can be restrictive.

DALL-E

DALL-E is also user-friendly but shares similar limitations with Midjourney in terms of cost and model variety. Creating the desired output often requires multiple iterations, likely due to the restrictions on generation options.
